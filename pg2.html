Education/Requirements

Education

You have to have a solid foundation knowledge of chemistry or science. Usually a perfumer would have a bachelor's degree in chemistry, biology, or a related field. Some perfumers took specialized perfume schools to learn how to use raw materials; marketing and branding; and creating a fragrance.

Requirements

You are required to know chemical properties; blending and formulating; and how scents react in different products like perfumes v.s. soap. Another required skill is business skills like marketing, communication, and project management. The other two most important skill is creativity and a keen nose or strong sense of smell.
