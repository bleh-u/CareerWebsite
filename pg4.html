Employers

Givaudan

Givaudan is one of the largest manufacturers of fragrances. They work with many leading brands such as Calvin Klein, Avon, Davidoff, and many more. If you are interested in working with big brand names, they are the perfect pick.

SOB Fragrance

They are a prominent fragrance manufacturer for fragrance products. SOB specializes in personalized fragrances or creating personal smells for their clients. If you are interested in experimenting and creating unique scents that suit customer's identities, this company would be perfect.

IFF Fragrance

This is a leading scent company as well. They provide new scents for small, medium, and large companies/global brands. As a perfumer there you are allowed to enter their greenhouse to study aromas as a benefit. If you are interested in seeing and smelling exotic flowers, this company gives you the perfect chance to do so.
